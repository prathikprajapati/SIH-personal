Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SecureErase ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our data erasure services and website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This policy applies to all visitors and users of our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, company information, and contact details provided during registration or service requests
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website and services, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited
  • Service Information: Details about the devices you wish to erase, service requests, and certificate information

We do not collect sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary for providing our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process your data erasure requests
  • To communicate with you about your account or services
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud and protect our security

Data Retention & Erasure

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements:

  • Account information: As long as your account is active
  • Service records: 5 years after service completion
  • Communication logs: 2 years after last interaction

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information at any time. Upon request, we will securely delete your data in accordance with applicable laws.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure server infrastructure and network monitoring

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to assist in providing our services:

  • Cloud hosting providers for service delivery
  • Email service providers for communication
  • Analytics services for website improvement

These third parties are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information and use it only for the purposes specified in our agreement with them.

Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how your data is used
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of our services after any modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What personal data do you collect?
We collect minimal personal information necessary for our services, including your name, email address, company details, and information about devices you wish to erase. We do not collect sensitive personal data beyond what is required for service delivery.
How long do you keep my data?
We retain your data only as long as necessary for providing our services. Account information is kept while your account is active, and service records are retained for 5 years after completion. You can request deletion of your data at any time.
Can I request deletion of my data?
Yes, you have the right to request deletion of your personal information. We will securely delete your data in accordance with applicable laws upon receiving your request. Please contact us at privacy@secureerase.com to initiate this process.
How do you protect my data?
We implement robust security measures including encryption, access controls, regular security audits, and secure server infrastructure. All data is protected using industry-standard security protocols to ensure your information remains confidential and secure.
